# Content Warnings

This book contains mature themes that may not be suitable for all readers:

- **Childhood abuse & parental exploitation:** The protagonist was used as a test subject by her own parents throughout childhood and adolescence.
- **Medical experimentation / non-consensual procedures:** Detailed depictions of clinical exams, invasive testing (including internal/reproductive exams), blood draws, and experimental procedures.
- **Body modification / genetic engineering:** Descriptions of human-animal chimera modifications including wolf ears, tail, fangs, and altered physiology.
- **Reproductive themes & estrous cycles:** Explicit discussion of non-human reproductive cycles, "heat" periods, overwhelming biological urges, and loss of bodily autonomy.
- **Dehumanization & objectification:** The protagonist is repeatedly treated as a specimen, asset, or experiment rather than a person.
- **Psychological trauma:** Flashbacks, dissociation, and ongoing effects of childhood trauma are depicted throughout.
- **Corporate control & coercion:** Themes of institutional power over the protagonist's body and choices.
- **Implied sexual content:** References to mating urges, physical intimacy, and sexual tension.

Reader discretion is strongly advised.
